Artist Statement

In my work, I explore the dynamic changes of our time, mirroring the evolution of human perception and its connection to the cosmos. Through painting, I bring intangible experiences into physical form.

I create intergalactic landscapes, depicting planets and beings made of pure energy. These works offer a glimpse into realities beyond the material world. By painting these realms, I invite viewers to reflect on the unseen forces shaping their own lives and recognize that energy and consciousness extend far beyond what we perceive.

My work serves as a visual bridge between the material and the unseen. In a world of rapid change, my paintings offer a space for reflection, inviting viewers to embrace new perspectives and reconnect with the vastness of existence.

My creating process is both structured and fluid—I begin with a vision, allowing materials and colors to guide me as the painting evolves, creating a dialogue between intention and intuition, where movement and layering subtly lead the viewer toward deeper perception.

At its core, my work is an invitation to expanded awareness, encouraging a deeper understanding of the hidden forces shaping reality and our connection to something far greater than ourselves.

About

Born in 1995 in Baku, Azerbaijan, Lya Helia is a Bali-based, self-taught abstract artist who explores the dynamic changes of our time, mirroring the evolution of human perception and its connection to the cosmos. Her distinctive style conveys dynamic and evocative color schemes through mediums such as watercolor, acrylic, collage, and digital illustration, capturing the spectrum of human emotions.
