Deconstructions

← Back to Portfolio

Deconstructions (2025) is an experimental series of AI-assisted animations based on ink sketches made between 2023 and 2025. Each piece deconstructs a specific psychological framework I was intensely experiencing at the time. The project uses generative tools to rework static drawings into animated sequences, exploring the medium itself while translating inner structures into movement, texture, and rhythm.
