Symbolic Landscapes (2022) is a series of analog collages made from fragments of bodies, architecture, and natural elements. The works explore spatial relationships between organic and built environments. The focus is on form and rhythm, with an intention to convey the meaning behind the names of natural places—mountains, rivers—through composition, contrast, and the interplay of shapes.
