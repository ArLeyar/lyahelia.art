Emotional Postcards (2022) is a watercolor series exploring how abstract form, color, and composition can encode states of mind. Painted without preparatory sketches, the works rely on intuitive response to material and moment and function as a fragment of lived experience.

Total number of works: 48, all unique. Sold out.
