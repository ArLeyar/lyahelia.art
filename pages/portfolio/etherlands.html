Etherlands

← Back to Portfolio

Etherlands (2023-2025) is a fictional dimension expressed through painting and installation. It turns cognitive patterns of perception into visual forms. Each work captures a moment or state through materials like watercolor, ink, acrylic, and video. The project is divided into five parts: dwellers, terrains, anomalies, transmissions, and arrivals. Together, they form a system for sensing what usually stays unseen.

Self that sees itself (in Etherlands) video essay extend Etherlands into a time-based medium, translating its forms and logic into movement, rhythm, and voice. It deconstructs private experience into fragments offering a way to reassemble emotion and perception through experimental narrative. On view in Artfarm Pilaster, Bonavigo, Italy.
