Textures

← Back to Portfolio

Textures (2022-2025) is a series exploring tactility and the transition from 2D to 3D. Each work emerges from a persistent investigation into the physical limits of canvas by stretching, layering, folding, and reshaping it as a material body. It's a study in how surface can become structure.
