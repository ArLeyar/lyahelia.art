About

Lya Helia is an Azerbaijani artist based in Bali, Indonesia. Working with watercolor, ink, acrylic, and mixed media, she explores how unseen psychological patterns and perceptual mechanisms shape individual and collective experience. Through abstract forms and finely attuned color relationships, she constructs environments that reflect shifts in cognition, consciousness, and internal architecture.
