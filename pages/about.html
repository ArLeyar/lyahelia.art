About

Lya Helia is a Bali-based artist working with abstract painting and mixed media. Her practice explores the transformation of identity and the possibility of rendering the metaphysical into the physical. Using a perceptual approach and color as an instrument of awareness, she treats painting as a trace of inner experience and a dialogue with the unconscious.

Held in private collections worldwide, including the Netherlands, US, UK, Turkey, Indonesia, UAE, and Azerbaijan.
